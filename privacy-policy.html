Privacy Policy

Vibly — your warm, daily mood companion

Last updated: May 26, 2026

At Vibly, your privacy is at the heart of everything we build. This policy explains — in plain language — what information Vibly handles, how it's used, and your choices.

Your mood data never leaves your device. Vibly has no accounts, no cloud storage, and no advertising. Everything you journal stays private to you.

1. What data Vibly collects

Data stored on your device only

The following data is saved locally on your phone and never uploaded to any server:

  • Mood check-ins — your emotion, energy, and focus ratings (1–10 scale).
  • Journal entries — any text you write in your private journal.
  • "One Bright Thing" notes — a short note about what went well each day.
  • Mood tags — any context tags you choose (Work, Sleep, Health, etc.).
  • App preferences — your reminder time, sound settings, and subscription status.

Data processed by AI (temporary, not stored)

When Vibly generates a Simple Action suggestion or a Daily Insight, your recent mood numbers (emotion, energy, focus) and optional mood tags are sent temporarily to an AI service (Anthropic Claude, via Rork's secure toolkit proxy). This data is:

  • Used only to generate your personalized suggestion or insight.
  • Not stored by the AI provider after the request completes.
  • Not linked to your identity — no name, email, or device ID is attached.
  • Optional — Simple Actions and Daily Insights are additional features; you can skip or refresh them anytime.

Subscription data (RevenueCat)

If you subscribe to Vibly Premium, your subscription is managed by RevenueCat, a trusted third-party subscription platform. RevenueCat receives:

  • An anonymous app-level user ID (not your name or email).
  • Your subscription status (active, expired, trial).

RevenueCat does not receive any of your mood data, journal entries, or personal information. See RevenueCat's privacy policy.

2. What Vibly does NOT do

  • No accounts — Vibly does not require sign-up, login, or any personal identifiers.
  • No cloud storage — your journal and mood data are stored only on your device.
  • No advertising — Vibly has no ads and no ad trackers.
  • No analytics trackers — Vibly does not use Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any third-party tracking.
  • No data selling — your data is never sold, shared, or used for any purpose beyond providing the app's features.

3. Notifications

Vibly offers an optional daily reminder to check in on your mood. This notification is scheduled locally on your device. You can enable or disable it anytime in Settings. No notification data is sent to any server.

4. Children's privacy

Vibly is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us and we will delete it.

5. Your rights & control

Since all your data lives on your device, you have full control:

  • Delete anytime — uninstalling Vibly removes all your data permanently.
  • No data to export — since Vibly has no cloud accounts, there is no "account data" to request.
  • Opt out of AI — the AI suggestions and insights are optional; you can simply not use them.
  • Control notifications — toggle reminders on/off in Settings at any time.

6. Third-party services

Vibly integrates with these services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • RevenueCat — revenuecat.com/privacy (subscription management)
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — anthropic.com/legal/privacy (AI-generated suggestions, via Rork's secure proxy)
  • Apple / Google — standard app store policies apply when you download or subscribe through the App Store or Google Play.

7. Changes to this policy

If we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Major changes may be highlighted within the app. Continued use of Vibly after changes means you accept the updated policy.
