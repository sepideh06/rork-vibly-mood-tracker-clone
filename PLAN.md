# Vibly — a warm, AI-powered daily mood companion

## First impression
Opening Vibly should feel like watching a sunrise — warm peach, coral and amber gradients drifting softly behind the content. Calming, optimistic, personal. Every tap should feel buttery with subtle haptics and gentle motion.

## Plan & pricing
- **3-day free trial** — every feature unlocked from day one.
- After the trial, users subscribe to keep using Vibly:
  - **Monthly** — €5 / month
  - **Yearly** — €45 / year (saves ~35%)
- When the trial ends, the paywall is shown until the user picks a plan.

## Features (all included)
- **Daily Check-In** — emoji-led emotion picker plus smooth animated sliders for Energy and Focus, with the background hue subtly shifting as you drag.
- **One Bright Thing** — a quick note for what went well today, gently saved as a memory.
- **Journal** — a dedicated tab where you can keep adding short reflections with a "+" button.
- **Mood Dashboard** — a recent-days mood ribbon, animated trend chart, and quick stats (average mood, best day, current state).
- **Simple Action** — one AI-generated suggestion (exercise, mindset shift, or rest cue) you can refresh anytime to get a new idea.
- **Streaks & gentle reminders** — daily streak counter and a friendly nudge toggle.
- **Creative energy insight** — a daily, warmly written read on your creative rhythm, peak hours and pace, refreshed each day.
- **Mood tags** — tag entries with Work, Sleep, People, Health, Movement, Creativity for richer trends.
- **Generated app icon** — a warm peach-to-coral gradient circle with a soft, glowing wave/heartbeat mark.

## Design
- **Palette**: peach, coral, soft amber, with cream surfaces and a deep plum text color for contrast.
- **Backgrounds**: layered sunset gradients with faint blurred orbs that drift, giving depth without noise.
- **Typography**: an editorial serif for headings paired with a clean modern sans for body — distinctive, not the usual AI default.
- **Motion**: spring-based slider thumbs, number counters that tick, chart bars that rise on mount, button press scale + haptics.
- **Cards**: rounded, soft cream cards with delicate shadows and a thin warm border.

## Screens
- **Today** — greeting, current check-in summary, today's Simple Action card with refresh, One Bright Thing note, journal shortcut, streak chip.
- **Check-In** — emoji-based emotion picker, animated Energy and Focus sliders, optional mood tags, save celebration.
- **Journal** — list of past entries plus a "+" button to add new short reflections.
- **Insights (Dashboard)** — mood ribbon for the last 14 days, trend chart, key stats, creative energy insight, mood-by-context breakdown.
- **Profile / Settings** — streak display, reminder toggle, subscription status (trial countdown or active plan), about.
- **Paywall** — warm, focused screen with monthly & yearly plan options and a single subscribe CTA, shown automatically after the trial ends.
